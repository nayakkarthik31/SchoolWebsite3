Facilities/Campus life

Cafeteria

At Inventure Academy, mealtimes are rife with fellowship and learning, an atmosphere of community feeling and interaction. The large and well-ventilated Cafeteria seats the entire school in three batches. Children are served wholesome, freshly cooked, tasty dishes, both vegetarian and non-vegetarian, with a varied menu so that different palates are satisfied. The food is hygienically prepared in the kitchen attached to the dining hall. Children have breakfast and lunch as well as a mid-morning health drink and a post-school snack and juice. Children not on the Meal Plan also eat in the cafeteria.

The Hub

A generous, flexible and multipurpose 40,000 sq ft space with amphitheatre seating, green rooms and backstage areas. Our annual musical productions, the Round Square Youth Parliament, Makkala Habba and the Graduation and Moving Up Ceremonies are all held in this vibrant space. The Hub is also home to two new basketball courts, Maker Space and Design & Tech studio (4175 sq ft), as well as custom built spaces for art (5000 sq ft), music and dance (4180 sq ft combined).

Makerspace

A large, innovators lab where children make things to learn things. And they learn so much: finding solutions for everyday problems through design thinking; acquiring technical skills and strategic thinking; making mistakes and dealing with the fallout, both practical and emotional; trying things a hundred different ways; using tools from corrugated cardboard for prototypes to software for 3D modeling and much more. And through it all, the focus is the discovery and making process to nurture the inventors of the future.

Nurse

Inventure Academy has tied up with Columbia Asia Hospital units in Whitefield and Sarjapur Road, and we have a qualified nurse at school full-time from Vita Life for medical support. We also have a doctor on call on all working days. Our campus clinic is well equipped to handle any emergency medical situation that may arise.

Sports

We have a Soccer Field with Artificial Turf, a Swimming Pool 25 m long with attached changing rooms; also has a toddlers pool, 5 Tennis Courts (Clay and Hard), Cricket Nets with Practice Pitches, a Gym, an Athletics Field with a Cricket Pitch, a Basketball Court, a Yoga Gazebo and a Preschool Play area

Transport

Inventure Academy has an efficient and reliable transport system with numerous buses and bus stops. Our transport provider is Baghirathi Transport Services. Their Visible Mobile App facilitates monitoring of your child’s travel to and from school. Every bus has a CCTV and a female caretaker. Students are expected to maintain proper decorum on the bus and must follow a prescribed set of rules that regulate their behaviour.

Achievements

Ranks

Inventure is ranked #1 in Karnataka & Bangalore and #3 in India in the Education World C Fore Survey 2020 (Co Ed Day Schools Category). Inventure has ranked amongst the Top 10 schools in India for the last three years.

Colleges

Inventurers have got admissions in leading colleges across India & the rest of the world, including Harvard, Stanford, University of California Berkeley, University of Pennsylvania, Brown University, Carnegie Mellon University, Cambridge University, Harvey Mudd, Georgia Tech, National Law University, JIPMER Pondicherry and Ashoka University.

Changemaker Challenge

Inventure received ‘Most Innovative Practice’ award by Tata Communications and Parikrma Humanity Foundation for its Changemaker Program in 2018 and ‘Most Innovative Idea’ award for its Empathy Program in 2019.(The Changemaker program teaches students how to be advocates of positive change in their communities. The Empathy Program is aimed at helping students better understand themselves and their emotions, be more empathetic towards others, nature and their environments, as well as their communities.)

Round Square

Inventure is a part of Round Square, a select group of 200 schools worldwide which facilitates student and faculty exchange opportunities & learning / sharing of best practices globally.

Faculty

  • Training @ Inventure

    At Inventure Academy, we want our teachers to flourish. This includes redesigning and upgrading their practises, developing critical thinking, learning styles, classroom management, design thinking, team building and more. Our teachers experience 16-17 days of training and professional development in a year, including the annual outbound programme. Senior teachers mentor newcomers, reinforcing the values of the school through peer to peer engagement. This year, we have introduced a Train the Trainer programme. A Teacher Leader programme is also in place for people managers. Apart from the inhouse training programmes, teachers also attend several workshops conducted by the different boards of education like CISCE and CIE and international conferences like the 21st Century Learning Conference, Round Square Conference etc.

  • Guest Faculty

    Founded by alumni of XLRI, INSEAD and IIM, INME is a leading outbound learning organization focusing on building life and leadership skills in children as well as professionals. Good teachers make better students – led by this philosophy we invest in the development of our faculty with the help of INME’s faculty development programmes. The objective is to help teachers plan and act in a way that delivers on the school’s core purpose, values and vision. For students, the curriculum is designed to develop age-relevant life skills and student teacher bonding, while giving them an opportunity to experience the diversity of India through outbound class trips (grades 4-12). Students get certified at each level of the program.
